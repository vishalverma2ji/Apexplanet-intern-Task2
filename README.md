🚗 Car Sales Performance Dashboard

Data Analytics Internship Project – Task 2 (EDA & Business Intelligence)

---

📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes 472,000+ used car sales records to uncover key business insights related to revenue performance, brand dominance, pricing trends, and regional sales distribution.

The objective was to apply SQL for business problem solving and build an interactive Power BI dashboard to support data-driven decision-making.

---

🎯 Business Objective

To answer critical business questions such as:

- Which brands generate the highest revenue?
- Which states dominate used car sales?
- How does vehicle mileage impact selling price?
- What body types contribute most to total sales?
- What are the yearly revenue trends?

---

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

- MySQL – Data extraction, aggregation, and business queries
- Power BI – Interactive dashboard creation
- DAX – KPI calculations and performance measures
- Data Modeling – Structured data for reporting

---

📊 Key KPIs Created

- Total Revenue
- Total Vehicles Sold
- Average Selling Price
- Average Vehicle Condition

---

📈 Dashboard Highlights

The interactive dashboard includes:

- 🔹 Top Revenue by State
- 🔹 Top Revenue by Brand
- 🔹 Revenue Trend by Year
- 🔹 Sales Distribution by Body Type
- 🔹 Odometer vs Selling Price Analysis
- 🔹 Dynamic Filters (Brand, Model, State, Transmission)

---

🔍 Key Insights

- Total Revenue exceeded 6 Billion, indicating strong resale market activity.
- Premium brands generate higher revenue despite lower sales volume.
- Florida and California are top-performing states.
- Sedans and SUVs dominate the market share.
- Vehicles with lower mileage sell at significantly higher prices.
- Selling price shows strong dependency on vehicle condition and odometer reading.

---

🧠 Business Impact

This dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- Identify high-performing brands and regions
- Track revenue growth trends over time
- Understand pricing drivers
- Make strategic inventory and pricing decisions
